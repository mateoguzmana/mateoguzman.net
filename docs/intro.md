# About

I'm Mateo Guzmán — a senior mobile engineer based in The Netherlands. Most of my last decade has been React Native: shipping apps to millions of users, [contributing to the core project](./open-source), and writing publicly about the gnarly performance and bundle-size problems that don't have easy answers.

Currently full-time at Bitvavo and a [core contributor](https://github.com/facebook/react-native/pulls?q=author%3Amateoguzmana) to facebook/react-native — Image caching for Android, the Java-to-Kotlin migration of the Android codebase, reducing the public Android API surface, and a few smaller things. On the side I maintain [`react-native-lz4`](https://github.com/mateoguzmana/react-native-lz4) and [`react-native-fiesta`](https://github.com/mateoguzmana/react-native-fiesta), and I [write](/blog) about the trickier corners of the platform.

Originally from Medellín, Colombia. Outside work I read a lot about space exploration — which is partly why one of my [personal projects](./personal-projects) is [Xplorer](https://xplorerapp.net/), a planetary-exploration app with AI-generated planet textures. [Traveling](./travelling) is the other thing that pulls me out of the keyboard; living in Europe makes that easy.

If anything here is useful or you'd like to compare notes on a hard problem, [drop me a line](mailto:info@mateoguzmana.net).
